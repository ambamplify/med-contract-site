Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What types of IM & hospitalist contracts does this analyze?
All IM and hospitalist employment contracts — national staffing companies, regional staffing groups, hospital direct, democratic groups, and regional staffing companies. Works for RVU-based, hourly, salary, hybrid, and collections-based compensation structures.
Is this a substitute for a healthcare attorney?
No. This tool provides data-driven financial and structural analysis against market benchmarks. It identifies what to negotiate and by how much. For contracts with High or Critical risk ratings — particularly non-compete clauses or complex RVU formulas — we recommend consulting a healthcare attorney. We provide referrals to AHLA member attorneys in your state.
How accurate are the compensation benchmarks?
Benchmarks are sourced from MGMA 2025 + AMGA 2025 + Doximity 2025 + SHM 2024 benchmarks. We use work RVU (wRVU) benchmarks specifically — not total RVUs — and break them down by employer type (staffing company, democratic group, hospital direct) because conversion factors differ significantly across employer types.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes. The analyzer is fully mobile-optimized. On iPhone, save your contract PDF to the Files app and tap to upload. On Android, save to Downloads and select from your file browser. You can also paste contract text directly.
What happens to my contract data?
Your contract is processed securely for analysis and not shared with third parties. It is not used for any purpose beyond delivering your analysis. Results are tied to your analysis session and email for PDF delivery.
What is the $97 payment for?
One-time payment for a single contract analysis. Includes the full report, counter-proposal letter generator, 15 Q&A questions, and PDF download and email delivery. No subscription, no recurring charges.
